Button systems — Mono / minimal green

Five directions. Each shows Primary, Secondary, Ghost in resting state, plus a row showing the hover state (the live page also responds to real hover, press, and keyboard focus). A “View order” icon button shows the small/table size.

Option 1

Solid (refined)

Closest to a classic SaaS look: a confident solid-green primary, a green-tinted secondary, and a hairline ghost. Hover deepens the green; press nudges down 1px.

Soft tint (most minimal green)

Green is never a solid block — the primary is a soft green tint with green text, gaining a border only on hover. Quietest, most “minimal green” of the set; ghost is borderless until hovered.

Outline-fill (interactive)

Everything is an outline at rest; hover fills the button (green for primary, ink for secondary). Very crisp and tactile — the fill animation makes interactions feel responsive.

Option 4

Mono ink → green on hover

Purest Mono: the primary is black at rest and turns green only on hover, so green appears as a reward for interaction. Secondary is an ink outline that goes green on hover. Maximum restraint.
