# Send Invoice for Basecamp

Keep your team in the loop automatically. When Send Invoice emails an invoice for
a Shopify order, it can post an update to a **Basecamp project's Message Board** —
so everyone sees what went out, to whom, and for how much, without leaving Basecamp.

## What you get
- **Automatic updates** — every invoice you send from Send Invoice posts a tidy
  summary (store, invoice number, amount, recipient, date) to the project you choose.
- **Pick any project** — connect once and choose the destination project from a
  live dropdown of your Basecamp projects.
- **Secure by design** — Send Invoice connects through Basecamp's official OAuth 2
  flow. Your access tokens are encrypted at rest, and you can disconnect anytime.

## How to set it up
1. In Send Invoice, go to **Settings → Notifications**.
2. In the **Basecamp** card, click **Connect Basecamp** and approve access on the
   Basecamp (37signals) authorization screen.
3. Choose the **Project** whose Message Board should receive updates.
4. Toggle the Basecamp channel **on** and click **Send test update** to confirm.

That's it — from now on, sending an invoice also posts an update to that project.

## Requirements
- A Basecamp account and a project with a **Message Board**.
- The Basecamp user who connects must have permission to post to that project.

## Managing the connection
- Change the destination project anytime from **Settings → Notifications**.
- Click **Disconnect** in the Basecamp card to revoke access and stop updates.
